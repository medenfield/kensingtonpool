Directions

Here are some additional directions to help you find the pool.  The pool is located off the street in a park behind some houses and is difficult to see from the road.

Coming into town from the west on DeRenne/516: Turn right onto Habersham Street. Take the second left (at the flashing yellow light) onto Kensington Drive. At the intersection of Althea and Kensington, turn right onto the dirt/gravel road. It will take you into the pool parking area. The pool is located behind 249 Kensington Dr.

Coming into town from the east on DeRenne: Turn left onto Waters Avenue. Take the next right onto Althea Parkway in Kensington Park. Stay on Althea until it ends. Cross over Kensington Dr. and travel down the dirt/gravel road into the pool parking area. The pool is located behind 249 Kensington.

Feel free to call 912-352-0256 *during pool season* if you need any additional information about how to find us.

NOTE - The pool is on Althea Ct. which is an unmarked gravel road beside 249 Kensington.
