About Kensington Park Pool

 

Kensington Park Pool was established in the 1950's as a private pool for residents of the neighborhood.  In the 1980's we opened our membership to all interested residents of Chatham County. We have a 120 thousand gallon pool which goes from 3-9 ft. and a large baby pool separately enclosed with it's own deck and tables.  We are one of the few remaining facilities with a diving board.
